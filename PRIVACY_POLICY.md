# Privacy Policy for AutoHub

**Last Updated: December 15, 2025**

---

## Introduction

AutoHub ("we", "our", or "us") operates the AutoHub mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our App.

By using AutoHub, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

---

## Information We Collect

### 1. Personal Information

When you register for an account, we collect:

- **Name**: Your full name
- **Email Address**: For account creation and communication
- **Phone Number**: For contact purposes
- **Profile Picture**: Optional photo you upload
- **Password**: Encrypted and securely stored

### 2. Car Listing Information

When you create a car listing, we collect:

- Car details (title, description, price, category, specifications)
- Car images
- Location information (city/state you enter)
- Listing metadata (creation date, view counts)

### 3. Usage Data

We automatically collect:

- Device information (model, operating system)
- App usage statistics (features used, time spent)
- Error logs and crash reports
- Search queries and filter preferences

### 4. Chat Messages

We collect and store:

- Messages you send and receive
- Timestamps of messages
- Typing indicators
- Online/offline status

### 5. User Interactions

- Cars you favorite/save
- Your reviews and ratings
- Price alerts you set
- Notification preferences

---

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for:

### 1. **Providing Services**

- Creating and managing your account
- Displaying your car listings to potential buyers
- Enabling chat between buyers and sellers
- Processing searches and filters

### 2. **Communication**

- Sending notifications about messages, favorites, and price alerts
- Responding to your support requests
- Sending important updates about the App

### 3. **Improving the App**

- Analyzing usage patterns to enhance features
- Fixing bugs and technical issues
- Optimizing performance

### 4. **Security**

- Detecting and preventing fraud
- Enforcing our Terms of Service
- Protecting user safety

## Data Storage

### Firebase Services

We use Google Firebase for data storage:

- **Firebase Authentication**: Manages user accounts
- **Cloud Firestore**: Stores user profiles, car listings, reviews, favorites
- **Firebase Storage**: Stores uploaded images
- **Realtime Database**: Stores chat messages and real-time data

**Data Location**: Your data is stored on Firebase servers, which may be located in various regions worldwide.

**Data Retention**:

- Active accounts: Data retained indefinitely while account is active
- Deleted accounts: Data removed within 30 days
- Chat messages: Retained until manually deleted by users

## Data Sharing and Disclosure

### We DO NOT sell your personal information to third parties.

### We share information only in these cases:

1. **Public Listings**: Car listings you create are visible to all app users
2. **Chat Partners**: Messages are shared only with the specific user you're chatting with
3. **Legal Requirements**: If required by law or legal process
4. **Service Providers**: With Firebase/Google for app infrastructure
5. **Safety**: To protect rights, property, or safety of users

- **Encryption**: Passwords are encrypted using industry-standard methods
